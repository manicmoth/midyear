Mid-year Report

Kate M. & Alex. B

Problem Space

Interactive art is an emerging domain of media that currently lacks tools that are approachable for entry level users and established artists.

The latter by opportunity cost

The former is typically barred by financial cost

Customer Description

Established Artist

Looking for a path into the interactive AR space

Early in Career (EIC) Artist

Trying to start out, doesn’t have experience with many industry tools

Hobbyist

Primary goal is learning and scraping together projects

Proposed Solution

Smaller feature set than many industry standards

More affordable and easier to learn

Focused in on interactivity

Integrating physical world & digital creations to make projected art

Simulations and Live View of end result enables easier workflow

Workflow Prototype

Scan

Bringing 3D geometry into the virtual environment

Identify

Choose key features between models and environment

Connect

User selects interactions between features from a interaction set

Direct

Using the model of the space, simulate how projections and interactions play out

Perform

Display the art!

Value of Solution

Reduce barrier to entry by being approachable, easier to learn than existing apps

Lower the cost barrier to entry to allow emerging artists to grow their skills and learn

Enable users to play with interactivity as a medium, innovate on new ways to interact with art

Risks and Issues

We are working on a stand-alone product. There may be more value focusing on plugins for existing products.

  • Despite the risk, this path provides more freedom for us to prioritize the features our market needs without extra baggage

The market is fragmented.

  • We are targeting an underserved segment that currently has few players – none of which are B2C focused
  • Risk of other players targeting this same segment

Summary of Status

  • Semester spent better understanding problem space
  • Clear market segment demographic
  • Developed a clear mission driven solution to problem
  • Currently have product idea, list of features, wireframe of workflow

Next Steps

  • Run our product mockups by users and accordingly adapt
  • Build out essential features of our product - 3d viewer, projection mapping
  • Integrate with other workflow tooling
